click 'shoot' button:
	-go into shoot mode
	
shoot mode:
	-crosshair follows mouse on tiles
	-click on 'shoot' button exits mode
	-left click executes shot and ends turn
	
crosshair:
	-if target not visible and in range change color
	
execute shoot:
	-only possilbe if target visible and in range
	-if hits player wins
